Introduction

As cases of Corona Virus started to grow rapidly in America and globally, we started to see the cancellation of large public gatherings. Me being the concert/ live show enthusiast I am, I immediately worried about how this would not only effect festivals and live shows but overall the music industry overall. At first I was going to do research for primary sources but I realized I live in a time where social media is heavily used. Not just by day to day people, but also by these artists that are just a fraction of what make the music industry. For the sake of this project I looked into how this effects; artists, tour crew, venue employees, and record labels themselves. I reached out to several artists and recieved enthusiastic responses from Dreamers Delight and The Data Waves.
